  • Here is my observation on network organizations: they will need a new, full-blown role of The Networker. #futureofwork (ctd.)
  • Their job will be to facilitate connections in the network by looking at people, projects and communication to find potential for synergies.
  • Because in a network (like in any big organization) it is no longer possible to be aware of what is being done all the time.
  • The networker will tap into the data feed that is created by project tools, public communication, status updates and work with this data.
  • But the will also get to know the people and their ideas, their motivation and what they are trying to achieve for the customer.
  • This calls for a deeper understanding of the subject matter. The Networker will be a role that's an especially good fit for comb-shaped ppl.
  • These are generalists, but with high skills in communication and empathy.
  • The Networker will be a highly valuable role. Because they create new opportunities from existing resources.
  • It's different from classical business development, because that focuses on the customer facing outside.
  • Network organizations are no longer ONE business entity but span multiple businesses.
  • The networker also needs to get familiar with partners and what they bring to the product and how they are achieving their contribution.
