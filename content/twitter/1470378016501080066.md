[@JVApen](https://twitter.com/JVApen) [@Media](https://twitter.com/Media) Yes,

add a `media="screen"` to your current stylesheets (so they are not used when
printing).

and add a new stylesheet to your &lt;head&gt;:

&lt;link href="/path/to/print.css" media="print" rel="stylesheet" /&gt;

Write all your print-specific styles to that file.
